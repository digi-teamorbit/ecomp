About E-Comp

Our Mission

Over the last 25 years, we’ve realized that in order to have the happiest customers, we needed to reimagine how to purchase and pay for business insurance from the ground up.

We’ve turned the typical long and complicated process upside down, into a fast, money saving and pleasing experience.

Our Story

We were founded by an insurance geek who loves helping others, but she was fed up with the highly complicated process of shopping, purchasing and over-paying for business insurance.

As a business owner herself, Shawn personally felt the pains when budgets were blown due to unexpected insurance premiums due, or when the cash flow crunch came during audit time… and vowed big change needed to happen.

Guided By Technology, Driven By Live Expertise

Using decades of best practices research plus todays most advanced InsureTech, we’ve leveraged technology to un-complicate the complicated BUT we’ve kept our people skills in the process.

Our founder worked with top insurance carriers and developers to create a fresh and convenient insurance shopping marketplace PLUS a way to eliminate the down payment, creating the “new normal” way to get and pay-as-you-go for your workers’ compensation insurance.

Our service and turn-around standards are the best in the business – you’ll never be sent to a “round robin” call center or to another country for service. The results to our customer first are happy customers and happy channel partners.

A Fresh Revelation

When you pay-as-you-go, you’ve simplified the way you pay for your coverage. You remove the requirement to make the annual down payment or deposit and your premium payments are based on your actual payrolls instead of estimates.

Do we need to remind you how painful your audit process is every year? With pay-go, your audit process is simplified and your financial exposure is greatly reduced because you pay premiums as you pay your people.

Its workers’ compensation re-imagined, fresh and easy.

Our Carriers

We shop the marketplace to find our customers the best price with the best coverages and because we work with more than 50 insurance carriers, we hardly ever have to say no.

We believe that the insurance carriers we represent are a direct reflection of our company, which is why we only work with the highest rated, best, and brightest carriers in the country.

States We Serve

We’ve got a national footprint, serving businesses across America (with the exception of Alaska and Hawaii)

How it works

The Nitty Gritty

Our InsureTech allows businesses to receive instant quotes online in minutes and pay their workers’ compensation premiums as they pay their employees through existing and secure insurance company and payroll company integrations.

Compared to estimating your payrolls for the upcoming year, pay-go relies on real-time payroll data to calculate your workers’ compensation premiums, resulting in more accurate premium payments and reduced annual audits.
